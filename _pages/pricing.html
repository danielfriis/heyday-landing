FAQ

How does Heyday work?

Heyday is not a journal and not a therapist. It's a completely new kind of interface aimed at enabling a place for self-discovery the improvement of mental well-being.

What is a session?

You get the most out of Heyday if you take the time to really engage in the conversation. A session is thus considered one uninterupted conversation, capped at 3 hours from the conversation starts.

How is my payment processed?

We process payments using Lemon Squeezy. Learn more here.

Is my conversation private and secure?

Your privacy means everything to us. We store your conversations using industry-standard AES-256 GCM encryption in a way where not even we would be able to retrieve your data, should you forget your password. Your data is decrypted when you sign in and sent unencrypted to OpenAI for processing. Unfortunately, this is the only way to make Heyday work right now, but our aim is to eventually have end-to-end encryption.

What if I don't like it?

Send an email to contact@heyday.so within 30 days of your purchase and we'll send you a refund. No questions asked.

FAQ

What is Heyday?

Heyday is a digital platform that combines the reflective practice of journaling with AI-driven therapy. It's designed to provide personalized support and insights, helping you understand and navigate your mental well-being.

How does the AI therapy work?

Our AI therapy uses advanced algorithms to analyze your journal entries and provide interactive feedback. It understands your emotions, patterns, and offers insights and suggestions based on your individual needs.

Is my information private and secure?

Absolutely. Privacy and security are our top priorities. Your journal entries and any data shared with Heyday are encrypted and kept completely confidential. We are committed to ensuring your personal space remains private.

How can Heyday help me?

Heyday helps by providing a space for reflection, offering insights into your thoughts and feelings, and suggesting actionable steps towards mental well-being. It's like having a personal therapist who's available whenever you need to talk.

Do I need any experience with journaling or therapy?

Not at all. Heyday is designed for everyone, regardless of your experience with journaling or therapy. Our platform is user-friendly and guides you through the process.

Is Heyday a replacement for professional therapy?

While Heyday offers therapeutic insights and support, it should not be considered a replacement for professional therapy. If you're experiencing severe mental health issues, we recommend seeking help from a licensed professional.

How often should I use Heyday?

You can use Heyday as often as you like. Some users find daily entries helpful, while others prefer using it as and when they need to. It's flexible to suit your personal preference and lifestyle.
